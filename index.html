Our story

A house, considered

Sabrina Christie's Interiors began as one house, restored slowly, room by room. We kept what worked, replaced what didn't, and got a little obsessive about door handles along the way.

Today we design homes for a living — working with workshops and tradespeople across the country to make rooms that are meant to be lived in, not just photographed.
